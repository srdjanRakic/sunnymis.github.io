Projects

Stack OnTop

We have all Google'd a programming question, found a relevant Stack Overflow post, and quickly scrolled half way down the page to find the accepted answer. We can all admit to not reading the poster's question. With Stack OnTop, the accepted answer is automatically moved to the top of the page; saving you from those time-wasting scrolls.

Binger

Binger allows you to keep track of the shows you are currently binge watching, the shows you've finished watching and those you want to watch next! Find shows to watch by browsing thousands of titles and seeing what your friends have watched.

Swoleciety

Swoleciety lets you browse through exercises, schedule your workouts, and track the sets and reps you performed each day. Coming Soon: View statistics of previous workouts
